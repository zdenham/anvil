# Deep Sea Novel — Decomposition

An original novel inspired by deep-sea submarine adventure. ~80,000 words across 25 chapters in 3 parts.

**Working title:** *The Abyssal Throne*

**Logline:** A marine biologist conscripted aboard a rogue submarine must earn the trust of its enigmatic captain before a collision between deep-sea mining corporations and something unknown in the ocean's deepest trenches forces impossible choices.

**Setting:** Near-future (2040s). Deep-sea mining is a trillion-dollar industry. The ocean remains largely unexplored.

**Tone:** Literary adventure. Wonder and dread in equal measure.

**Output directory:** `novel/`

## Structure

- **Part I — *Descent*** (Ch 1–8): Protagonist brought aboard, worldbuilding, outsider finding footing
- **Part II — *The Midnight Zone*** (Ch 9–18): Mission deepens, backstories surface, stakes escalate
- **Part III — *Pressure*** (Ch 19–25): Convergence, confrontation, climax

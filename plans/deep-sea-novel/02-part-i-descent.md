# 02 — Part I: Descent (Chapters 1–8)

Write the first eight chapters of *The Abyssal Throne*.

## Goal

Produce chapters 1 through 8 as individual files: `novel/chapters/01.md` through `novel/chapters/08.md`.

## Context

Read `novel/outline.md` and `novel/characters.md` for the full story plan and character profiles. Follow the chapter beats established in the outline.

## Requirements

- Each chapter should be approximately 3,000–3,500 words
- Write in third-person limited POV (primarily Maren's perspective, though the outline may designate other POV chapters)
- Establish the world, the characters, and the central tension
- End Part I on a strong turning point that propels readers into Part II
- Each chapter file should begin with a markdown header: `# Chapter N: [Title]`

## Writing Quality

- Literary adventure tone — vivid sensory description, especially the submarine interior and ocean environments
- Strong, distinct character voices in dialogue
- Pacing that balances worldbuilding with forward momentum
- Show, don't tell — especially for character relationships and tension
- Use the submarine setting for claustrophobia, wonder, and intimacy
- Avoid purple prose but don't shy from beauty when the ocean demands it

## Output

Write each chapter to its own file:
```
novel/chapters/01.md
novel/chapters/02.md
...
novel/chapters/08.md
```

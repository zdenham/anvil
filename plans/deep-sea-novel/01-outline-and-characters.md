# 01 — Outline and Characters

Create the foundational planning documents for *The Abyssal Throne*.

## Goal

Produce two files that will guide all chapter writing:

1. **`novel/outline.md`** — Chapter-by-chapter beats for all 25 chapters
2. **`novel/characters.md`** — Detailed character profiles

## Outline Requirements

For each of the 25 chapters, provide:
- Chapter number and title
- POV character (if applicable)
- Setting/location
- 3–6 beat points describing key events, revelations, and emotional turns
- Approximate word count target (~3,000–3,500 words each)

The outline should establish:
- A clear narrative arc across all three parts
- Rising tension and stakes
- Subplots that weave through the main story
- Thematic throughlines (humanity vs. nature, obsession, the unknown, corporate greed vs. wonder)

### Part structure:
- **Part I — *Descent*** (Ch 1–8): Protagonist Dr. Maren Lisle, a deep-sea biologist, is recruited/coerced aboard the *Polaris*, a state-of-the-art rogue submarine commanded by Captain Idris Wren. She's an outsider among a tight crew. Worldbuilding: the near-future ocean economy, the mining corporations, the unexplored trenches. End Part I with a discovery that reframes the mission.
- **Part II — *The Midnight Zone*** (Ch 9–18): The true mission emerges. Backstories surface — why Wren went rogue, what the corporations are hiding, what Maren's own past connects to. They descend deeper. Strange phenomena. Alliances shift. A betrayal mid-part. Stakes escalate toward a point of no return.
- **Part III — *Pressure*** (Ch 19–25): All threads converge. The *Polaris* reaches the deepest point. Confrontation with both human antagonists and the unknown. Maren must make an impossible choice. Resolution that earns its ending — not tidy, but true.

## Character Requirements

Create detailed profiles for at least 6 characters:

### Core cast:
- **Dr. Maren Lisle** — Protagonist. Marine biologist. Why she's valuable to the mission. Her past, her motivation, her arc.
- **Captain Idris Wren** — Commander of the *Polaris*. Charismatic, brilliant, morally complex. What drove him to go rogue. His obsession.
- **Reva Okafor** — First officer. Loyal to Wren but with her own code. The crew's conscience.
- **Jonah Szarka** — Chief engineer. Keeps the boat alive. Sardonic, practical. Hidden depths.

### Supporting cast (at least 2 more):
- A corporate antagonist or representative
- A crew member who becomes a wildcard or traitor
- Others as needed to serve the story

For each character:
- Full name, age, background
- Role on the *Polaris* (or outside it)
- Personality, speech patterns, distinguishing traits
- Motivation and secret/hidden dimension
- Arc across the novel (how they change)
- Key relationships with other characters

## Constraints

- The novel is original — do not copy plots from 20,000 Leagues, The Abyss, or other submarine fiction, though drawing inspiration from the genre is fine
- Keep the tone literary but accessible — this is an adventure novel with depth (pun intended)
- The "unknown" in the trenches should be ambiguous enough to sustain wonder — avoid cliche (no straightforward sea monsters or aliens)

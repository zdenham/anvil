# 03 — Part II: The Midnight Zone (Chapters 9–18)

Write chapters 9 through 18 of *The Abyssal Throne*.

## Goal

Produce chapters 9 through 18 as individual files: `novel/chapters/09.md` through `novel/chapters/18.md`.

## Context

Read the following before writing:
- `novel/outline.md` — full story plan and chapter beats
- `novel/characters.md` — character profiles
- `novel/chapters/01.md` through `novel/chapters/08.md` — Part I (your upstream dependency)

Maintain continuity with Part I. Characters should evolve consistently. Plot threads established in Part I must be developed.

## Requirements

- Each chapter should be approximately 3,000–3,500 words
- This is the longest part — 10 chapters — and must sustain momentum through the middle
- Deepen character backstories and relationships
- Escalate stakes progressively
- Include a significant betrayal or reversal mid-part
- End Part II at a point of no return — the crew committed to the final descent
- Each chapter file should begin with a markdown header: `# Chapter N: [Title]`

## Writing Quality

- Maintain the literary adventure tone from Part I
- This part can go darker — the midnight zone has no light
- Increase tension through confined spaces, dwindling resources, interpersonal conflict
- Balance action set pieces with quieter character moments
- Strange phenomena should build gradually — unsettling, then awe-inspiring, then terrifying
- Dialogue should reveal character under pressure

## Output

Write each chapter to its own file:
```
novel/chapters/09.md
novel/chapters/10.md
...
novel/chapters/18.md
```

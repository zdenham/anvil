# 04 — Part III: Pressure (Chapters 19–25)

Write chapters 19 through 25 of *The Abyssal Throne*.

## Goal

Produce chapters 19 through 25 as individual files: `novel/chapters/19.md` through `novel/chapters/25.md`.

## Context

Read the following before writing:

- `novel/outline.md` — full story plan and chapter beats
- `novel/characters.md` — character profiles
- `novel/chapters/01.md` through `novel/chapters/18.md` — Parts I and II (your upstream dependencies)

This is the conclusion. Every thread must be addressed. Maintain full continuity with everything that came before.

## Requirements

- Each chapter should be approximately 3,000–3,500 words
- Bring all narrative threads to convergence
- The climax should feel earned — built from everything established in Parts I and II
- Maren's impossible choice must have real weight — no easy answers
- The resolution should be satisfying but not tidy — literary, not Hollywood
- The "unknown" should be revealed enough to feel complete but retain some mystery
- Each chapter file should begin with a markdown header: `# Chapter N: [Title]`

## Writing Quality

- Maximum tension — the pressure is literal and metaphorical
- This part should move fast but not rush — every scene earns its place
- Character arcs should complete with specificity, not generality
- The final chapter needs a strong closing image/line
- Action sequences should be visceral — hull groaning, systems failing, darkness pressing in
- Emotional payoffs should land — if you set something up in chapter 3, it pays off here

## Output

Write each chapter to its own file:

```
novel/chapters/19.md
novel/chapters/20.md
...
novel/chapters/25.md
```
